Garuda Mall – One of the Best Malls in Bangalore

Garuda Mall is a multi-level 6-storey retail mall with a diverse selection of international and national clothing branded stores to accommodate all visitors. This is one of the oldest malls in the city and is centrally located near Brigade Road.
The mall is 75,000 square metres in size. Apart from shopping, the mall also promises a great dose of entertainment, with INOX multiplex featuring more than 1000 seats and 5 screens. After shopping, you can also enjoy different cuisines at the Q court – Global Kitchens. Garuda Mall also features the city's largest integrated parking complex.

10:00 am – 10:30 pm (Weekends)

Park Square Mall – One of the Best Malls in Bangalore

Park Square Mall is located within India's largest IT park, International Tech Park Bangalore (ITPB). The mall, with over 450,000 square feet of space, is a popular hangout and shopping destination for the working professionals in the region. The mall offers a wide range of food and beverage, entertainment, and fashion options catering to the needs of the bustling Whitefield region and neighbouring areas.
Park Square Mall also features a well-planned gaming centre called 'Amoeba' to attract the attention of young adults and children. For some downtime entertainment, the mall features PVR Aura Cinema, where one can watch Indian and foreign films.


Address Park Square Mall , ITPB main road, International Tech Park, Whitefield Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560066
Timings10:00 am – 11:00 pm
Entertainment optionsAmoeba Bowling, and PVR Aura Cinema
Dining options Hard Rock Cafe, Barbeque Nation, Subway, Pizza Hut, KFC, Burger King and more
Retail outletsKargha- The Khadi store, Bata, Go Colors, Zudio, and more
Nearest metro stationPattandur Agrahara metro station (Purple Line) of the Namma Metro

Orion Mall – One of the Best Malls in Bangalore

Orion Mall is Bangalore’s third-largest mall featuring a multiplex, bowling & gaming zone, multiple shopping areas, and food courts. The mall is a part of Brigade Gateway, which is a world-class integrated enclave housing the Sheraton Grand Hotel, World Trade Centre, Columbia Asia Hospital, and other buildings.
In addition to shopping, Orion Mall is the ideal location to watch the latest blockbuster movies at PVR Cinemas Multiplex, which has 11 screens and 2,800 seats. It also houses a massive 60,000 square feet food court called SaucePan, which houses some of the most popular food stalls.


Address Orion Mall, Brigade Gateway, 26/1, Dr Rajkumar Road, Malleshwaram, Bengaluru, Karnataka - 560055
Timings10:00 am – 10:00 pm
Entertainment optionsTime Zone, Funkey Monkey, Hallucinate and PVR

Bangalore Central Mall – One of the Best Malls in Bangalore

Bangalore Central Mall is a major shopping location in the city, offering everything from luxurious shops to everyday stores. The mall is a popular weekend hang out for both residents and visitors, as it offers plenty of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.
You don’t have to worry about parking as the mall has a well-maintained valet service. Besides, the mall is located close to public transport facilities, which makes it conveniently accessible from all parts of the city.
