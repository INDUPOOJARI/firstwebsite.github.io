1.The Ritz-Carlton

The Ritz - Carlton, Bangalore features a spa with luxury treatments that are a mix of Indian and Eastern healing traditions. There is also a salon and a swimming pool available. The hotel’s spacious rooms offer air conditioning, a seating area, a work desk and a flat-screen satellite TV. There is also a minibar, an electric kettle, and an en suite bathroom with Asprey of London bath amenities. The hotel also provides car rental and currency exchange services and has a sauna, fitness centre, business centre, and tour desk available. Located at the heart of Bangalore, the hotel is conveniently less than a kilometer (0.6 mi) away from attractions like Brigade Road, UB City, Garuda Mall, and Kanteerava Indoor Stadium.

2.ITC Windsor,a Luxury Collection Hotel

ITC Windsor is a Luxury Collection Hotel that boasts elegantly furnished rooms that come with free wifi, air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, tea- and coffee-making facilities, and an en suite bathroom with a bath and a hot/cold shower. The hotels offers a plethora of spa treatments, a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and butler service for guests’ convenience. The hotel has five different restaurants that specialize in different cuisines, ranging from authentic Indian cuisines to international dishes.

Being 33 km (20 mi) away from Bengaluru International Airport, the hotel is conveniently close to Bangalore Golf Course, National Gallery of Modern Art, Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, Bangalore Palace, and Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park.

3.Radisson Bengaluru City Center

Radisson Bengaluru City Center’s air-conditioned guest rooms feature a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, cosy bed linens, and a private bathroom with a shower and bathrobes. The hotel’s restaurant serves a continental breakfast every day. There is also an outdoor swimming pool and fitness centre available for guests’ leisure. The 24-hour front desk offers rooms service and currency exchange.

Some notable attractions near the hotel are Bangalore Central Mall and Ulsoor Lake which are less than a kilometer away (0.6 miles) from the hotel. The hotel is also 1.4 km (0.8 mi) from Commercial Street, 1.7 km (1 mi) from Brigade Road, 1.8 km (1.1 mi) from St. Mary’s Basilica, 2.2 km (1.3 mi) from Chinnaswamy Stadium, 2.9 km (1.8 mi) from Kanteerava Indoor Stadium, and 3.2 km (1.9 mi) from Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park.

4.Vivanta Bengaluru Residency Road

This lavish hotel boasts a spa, outdoor swimming pool, and 24-hour fitness centre. Its rooms are furnished with a minibar, tea- and coffee-making facilities, a flat-screen cable TV, and a safety deposit box. Some of these rooms also have great views of the swimming pool and the city. Guests can request secretarial or airport transfer services at the front desk.

Conveniently, this hotel is close to many attractions around Bangalore as it’s only less than 1.5 km (0.9 mi) away from Garuda Mall, Brigade Road, Bangalore Central Mall, St. Mark’s Cathedral, Commercial Street, Bangalore Aquarium, Chinnaswamy Stadium, and Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum.

5.Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel

Renaissance Bengaluru Race Course Hotel is a 5-star lifestyle property that offers wonderful views of the Bangalore Turf Club and features a 24-hour fitness centre, outdoor swimming pool, and a pool bar. Rooms come with a chic workstation, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a mini-fridge, tea- and coffee-making facilities, and a private bathroom with a hot tub. The hotel’s restaurant serves a buffet breakfast every day. Guests can also use the business facilities’ fax and photocopying services.

The hotel is close to many restaurants and attractions like Karnataka Chitrakala Parishath, Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park, Bangalore Palace, Visvesvaraya Industrial and Technological Museum, Mantri Square Mall, Freedom Park, Bangalore Golf Course, Jawaharlal Nehru Planetarium, and National Gallery of Modern Art.
